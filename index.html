About ICP

Indian Communist Party is a progressive communist party committed to democratic values and social equality. We believe in creating positive change through a revolution and for that we want to inspire the younger generation. Our party focuses on economic and social equality, sustainable development and the collective ownership of resources to ensure that wealth and power are distributed equitably among all members of society, fostering a classless and cooperative community.
