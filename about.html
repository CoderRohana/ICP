Our History

The Indian Communist Party (ICP) was established with the vision of creating a more equitable and just society in India. Our journey began with the mission to represent the working class and advocate for their rights and interests.

Founded on 15th January, 2024, ICP aims to revolutionize the communist party of India and make a more strong enough party to compete against other right wing parties of India.

Our Mission

The ICP is committed to building a socialist society based on the principles of equality, justice, and collective prosperity. We strive to eliminate exploitation in all its forms and ensure that the fruits of economic development reach every citizen.

Our mission includes fighting against economic inequality, social discrimination, and political oppression while promoting democratic values and secular principles in Indian society.

Core Values

Economic Justice

Working towards fair distribution of resources and economic opportunities for all citizens

Social Equality

Fighting against all forms of discrimination and promoting inclusive social development

Worker's Rights

Protecting and advancing the rights and interests of the working class

Marxism–Leninism

Strength through unity, guided by Marxist-Leninist principles, to defend the revolution and uphold the sovereignty of the proletariat.
