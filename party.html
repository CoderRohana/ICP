Party Structure and Leadership

Organizational Hierarchy

The Indian Communist Party operates through a democratic centralist structure, ensuring both democratic participation and organizational efficiency. Our structure allows for bottom-up participation while maintaining coordinated action across all levels.

Organizational Levels:

  • Central Committee
  • Politburo
  • State Committees
  • District Committees
  • Local Committees

Current Leadership

Our party leadership consists of dedicated individuals committed to the cause of socialism and social justice. Each leader brings unique expertise and experience to guide our movement forward.

Decision Making Process

The ICP follows a democratic centralist approach to decision-making, which ensures both democratic participation and organizational unity. Key features include:

  • Free and open discussion of all issues within the party
  • Democratic election of all leadership positions
  • Collective decision-making through party committees
  • Unity in action after decisions are made
